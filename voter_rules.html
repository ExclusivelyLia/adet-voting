Please be mindful of the following Voting Guidelines.

Eligibility

Only students enrolled in the College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS) are eligible to participate in the voting process.

One Person
One Vote

Each student can vote once for president, once for vice president, and six times for councilors per election. This ensures fairness and allows students to express their preferences without undue influence.

Confidentiality

The voting process must ensure confidentiality and anonymity. Individual voting choices should not be visible to anyone, including administrators, to maintain the integrity of the process.

Deadline

A clear voting deadline should be set and communicated to all students in advance. Once the deadline passes, the voting process should automatically close to prevent any further votes.
